Aptitude

"Aptitude is the natural ability or talent someone possesses in a particular skill or field, often demonstrated through proficiency, quick learning, and adept problem-solving."



Group discission

A group discussion is a collaborative conversation among multiple individuals on a specific topic or issue, aimed at sharing perspectives, exchanging ideas, and reaching consensus or understanding through active participation and respectful interaction.

Technical round

The technical round of a job interview typically involves assessing a candidate's proficiency in specific technical skills related to the position they are applying for. This round often includes questions, tasks, or scenarios designed to evaluate the candidate's knowledge, problem-solving abilities, and practical application of technical concepts relevant to the role.



HR Training

The HR round of a job interview is the stage where candidates interact with representatives from the Human Resources department of the company. This round focuses on assessing the candidate's suitability for the organization in terms of cultural fit, soft skills, interpersonal abilities, and alignment with the company's values and goals.

DESIGNING

Designing encompasses the creative process of conceiving and crafting solutions to address specific problems or fulfill particular needs. It involves applying principles of aesthetics, functionality, usability, and user experience to develop products, services, systems, or experiences that are visually appealing, practical, and effective.
